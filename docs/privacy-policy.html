CurrentNoise - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Overview

CurrentNoise ("we", "our", "the Service") is an automated content creation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

The Service collects minimal information necessary to operate:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens: When you connect your TikTok account, we store an access token and refresh token locally on your machine to publish videos on your behalf. These tokens are never transmitted to any third party.
  • Generated content: Videos, lyrics, and related files are generated and stored locally on your machine.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • TikTok profile information beyond what is needed for authentication
  • Information from your TikTok followers or viewers

4. How We Use Information

OAuth tokens are used solely to authenticate with the TikTok Content Posting API to upload and publish videos to your account. All processing happens locally on your device.

5. Data Storage

All data (tokens, generated content, configuration) is stored locally on your machine. No data is stored on external servers operated by CurrentNoise.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with third-party APIs to generate content:

  • TikTok API (video publishing)
  • AI music generation services (song creation)
  • AI video generation services (video clip creation)
  • News APIs (trending news retrieval)

Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing the application files. To revoke TikTok access, visit your TikTok account settings and remove the app authorization.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via our TikTok account: @currentnoise.
